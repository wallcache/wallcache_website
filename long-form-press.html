Long Form Press

"Read slowly or die trying."

A Brand for Readers Who Finish the Book

Long Form Press is a literary goods brand for people who take reading seriously. We make beautiful, thoughtful objects that respect the depth of the written word—not merchandise, but things that honor the patient work of finishing difficult books.

The ouroboros is our symbol: eternal return, cyclical reading, the endless conversation between writer and reader. It represents the commitment to finishing what you start and returning to the books that shaped you.

Our Philosophy

For Serious Readers

We design for people who finish Moby-Dick, who push through Ulysses, who return to The Brothers Karamazov. Readers who understand that great literature demands patience and rewards it.

Depth Over Speed

In a world obsessed with productivity and quick consumption, we celebrate slow reading. The kind that requires marginalia, rereading, and sitting with difficult passages until they reveal themselves.

Quality Objects

Our products are made with the same care and attention you bring to reading. Durable materials, thoughtful design, and craftsmanship that lasts—because great books deserve great things.

The Canon Matters

We believe in The Canon. Not as gatekeeping, but as a conversation across centuries. The books that have survived because they contain truths worth preserving. Worth struggling with. Worth finishing.

Products Coming Soon

We're currently developing our first collection of literary goods. From reading journals to book-finishing badges, from marginalia notebooks to ouroboros pins—each piece designed for readers who understand that finishing matters.

Join the community of readers who finish what they start.
