Summary

Analytical, creative, and quietly relentless, I'm a Data Engineer with a strong academic foundation in Theoretical Physics and hands-on experience building scalable data products across insurance, finance, and cloud transformation. Beyond work, I'm a voracious reader of fiction and philosophy, a long-distance runner, and a dog-powered hiker who's happiest roaming the wilder corners of the UK. Whether I'm owning a roadmap or navigating a mountainside in a gale, I bring the same mindset: stay curious, move with purpose, and leave things better than I found them.
