WeMetAtEight

Capturing connection at London's dating events

About the Project

WeMetAtEight brings a fresh approach to dating in London—structured events where strangers meet over conversation, games, and genuine connection. As event photographer, I document the energy, laughter, and authentic moments that emerge when people step away from apps and into real interaction.

The challenge is balancing documentation with discretion—capturing the atmosphere without intruding on intimate conversations. These images show the event's structure, the venue's ambiance, and the collective energy of people taking a chance on meeting someone new.
